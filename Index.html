Chris

Rowdy. Loyal. Shows up when it counts.

For his birthday, we’re collecting letters, stories, and memories to print into a keepsake book. Keep it real. One specific moment beats a long speech.

Write a note Use a prompt

1–2 pages is perfect. Short is welcome. Honest matters more than polished.

The Process

Simple on purpose.

1

Write

A letter, a memory, a moment. Keep it specific.

2

Collect

Submissions go privately to Kayla for formatting.

3

Print

Everything becomes a book he can keep.

What hits hardest: a real story, a real detail, a real reason you respect him.

Prompts

Pick one. Or ignore this and just write.

“A moment I saw your character clearly was…”
“You probably don’t realize it, but you changed ____ when you…”
“The thing I respect most about you is…”
“If someone asked me what kind of man you are, I’d say…”
“A funny ‘Classic Fighter’ moment I’ll never forget is…”

Write your note

This goes directly to Kayla and is used only for the printed birthday book.

Tip: one story + one thing you respect + one line you hope he remembers.
Email instead
