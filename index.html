What to write

  • A story that captures who he is

How this works

1) Write

Drop a letter, memory, or message below. Keep it honest and specific.

2) Collect

All entries are gathered privately by Kayla and formatted into a book.

3) Print

The final book becomes a keepsake he can revisit whenever he needs it.

Meaningful prompts (pick any)

Stories hit harder than praise

  • “A moment I saw your character clearly was…”
  • “You probably don’t realize it, but you changed ____ when you…”
  • “The thing I respect most about you is ____ because…”
  • “If someone asked me what kind of man you are, I’d say…”

For family / long-time friends

  • “From the early days to now, what’s stayed true about you is…”
  • “When things got hard, you handled it by…”
  • “A funny ‘classic Chris’ moment I’ll never forget is…”
  • “What I hope you never doubt about yourself is…”

Optional: end with a simple line like “Proud of you.” or “Still standing with you.”
